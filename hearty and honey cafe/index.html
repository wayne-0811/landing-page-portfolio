Our Story

We didn't set out to build a café. We set out to build a feeling.

It started in our grandmother's kitchen — the smell of cinnamon, the hiss of a kettle, the way a good meal could turn a hard day around. Hearth & Honey is our way of sharing that with you.

Everything on our menu is made by hand, in small batches, with ingredients from farms we actually know by name. No shortcuts, no fuss — just real food made by people who'd happily sit down and eat it with you.

Pull up a chair. Stay a while. You're always welcome here.

— Louise & Mateo, your hosts
